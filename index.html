FAQ

How does the app work, is everything safe?

Of course, everything is safe. Our loyal and excellent beta testers find all the shortcomings, and we correct them. Our application on other platforms may have a different structure of work, but the meaning and functionality does not change. If we talk about the general structure of the program, we can say that we use a triple matrix programmed in our programming language Acacia.

What is the difference between GoodModsLab and GoodLite?

The GoodModsLab version practically does not differ from the GoodLite version, the only difference is that some elements are removed in the lite version, but the main functionality is not limited by anything.

I'm a game developer, can my game be modified without my knowledge?

No, we act legally, we are against modding the game without permission, if of course there is an opportunity to contact the developers. Otherwise, we look at the community of games, whether they make modifications. Also on our platform you will never see cheats or utilities that break the mechanics of the game.

I noticed there are tabs of official mods and custom, what is it?

The list of official modifications is replenished by us, and you can add user lists if you are a mod developer.

What do I need to do to publish a mod to the platform?

You need to first become a verified user, that is, someone who can publish modifications.
To become one, you need to pass verification, but paid.

Can I suggest new mods or a list of supported games and apps?

Of course. We welcome every help, let us know which game or app you want to see modified.
