Appeal from the owner of GoodModsLab

Here soon there will be a message from the owner of the team.

From: Vlad K., Owner GoodModsLab
To whom the letter: Maxim Karpenko, Developer Super WorldBox
11.12.2022 17:15

Dear, Maxim Karpenko

On behalf of my GoodModsLab team, I am addressing you and your community. This message was written by me personally, no moderators touched it.

In this message I will describe everything about you. Let's start with the good side, you are a cool developer in the field of game development, it is not possible to argue with this. Your project is familiar to me from the very beginning, when only pixels were the houses of the NPCs. Our acquaintance was a dream that I was able to realize. Thank you very much for this!

However, our cooperation should also exist, because both of us have experience, both have a project that has no analogues. We must unite to resist all competitors out of spite.

Let's even recall the situation that happened in 2020 (If I'm not mistaken). When some unknown developers wanted to take away your project. Our GoodModsLab team notified their community about this and we all stood up for you!

Please, let's try a new cooperation, I think we will succeed thanks to our common efforts. Let me remind you about my team, in case you forgot - We are a team of developers who create various projects and modifications for games and applications. And we started our journey with your project. Due to my inexperience with big people (I'm talking about you, and your popularity) and illiteracy in my youth, I said all the bad things that should not have been. I wanted to show myself to you on an equal footing, and I'm still trying to look up to you.

Dear friend, colleague. Let's start a cool collaboration again?

I will briefly describe our cooperation:
• We will be cool suppliers of individual news to each other;
• My and the author's modifications are Ours (Unless, of course, the authors of the mods themselves will not mind).
• The community should be shared, because we have a community on both sides that is interested in your game (Even I play Super WorldBox when I have free time).
• We will share common experience in the development and promotion of our projects.
• I also don't mind joining your team, developing my main PET project in parallel.
